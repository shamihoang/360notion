General Questions

What is 360notion™?

360notion™ allows you to create a private social network for your company wherein you could:

  • chat with friends
  • share and backup files
  • post status updates
  • customize your profile
  • conduct performance evaluations

Why should I create a 360notion™ network for my company?

Companies are more productive if its members engage effectively with his/her colleagues.

360notion™ increases employee engagement by making it easier for members to share their ideas and experiences while the rest of the company gain new information and provide valuable feedback.

How can my company start using 360notion™?

To create a social network for your company, just sign up here.

How do I join my company's network?

To get a personal 360notion™ account, ask the responsible personnel to give you an invitation to your company's social network.

How safe is 360notion™?

360notion™ is safe and secure at all times. We can do this because:

  • Every session is secured with 2048-bit encryption, from login to logout regardless of device
  • We use multiple cloud storage providers to minimize potential disruptions
  • We do not store our members' financial data

How is 360notion™ different from free social networks and chat applications?

On free social networks and mobile chat applications, you cannot manage anything. This includes the exclusive right to profit from Intellectual Property you, your employees or your company communicates privately.
Moreover:

  • It's impossible to know or control what content people in your company are sharing and with whom
  • It's impossible to enforce employee accountability or conduct performance evaluations
  • Private messages on free social networks and chat applications are not private
  • Communications may not be secure or encrypted

Free social networks and mobile chat applications were not designed for the needs of companies!
In 360notion™:

  • You can use or assign admin roles and manage your company's network, create communities of interest (COIs), restrict file access and add or remove members.
  • Your company's Intellectual Property (IP) remains private and secured
  • We provide a wide variety of features so you can communicate and share based on context.


Pre-Sale Questions

Is there a trial period?

Yes! All companies get a 14-day trial period which is FREE of charge.

If you do not find 360notion™ useful during the trial period, you may cancel your subscription to remove your company from 360notion™ and you will not be charged anything.

How do i pay for this service?

You need a valid credit card to pay for your company's chosen plan. Payments are charged monthly

Will my credit card be charged immediately?

No! You won't be charged now. You'll only be charged if your company continues using a Mega or Premium Plan after your 14-day risk free trial ends.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept all MasterCard, Visa, Discover and American Express cards. Payments are charged monthly. You can cancel anytime. If you cancel, your company's data will be deleted from our database and no further payments are required.

What plan is best for my company?

If you are:

  • A startup with less than 3 members, you should get the Starter plan.
  • A small/medium size company with 3 to 50 members , you should get the Mega Plan for only $30/month per member.
  • A large company with 51 members or more, you should get the Premium Plan for only $25/month per member. This also costs less than a small cup of Starbucks® coffee per day!
Learn more about our pricing plan here.

Can I change my company's plan after signing up?

Yes! You may change your company's plan after signing up depending on your needs.

Can I opt-out from 360notion™?

Yes! You may opt-out any time if you already want to remove your company from 360notion™. After opting out, your company's data will be deleted from our database and you will not be charged anymore

Does 360notion™ have a referral program?

Earn 10% commission on referrals that result in a qualifying Mega or Premium Plan purchase. We will apply the 10% commission to your company’s 360notion™ account the following month.

There is a monthly $1000 referral credit limit per company.

How can 360notion™ save my company's resources?

First, you save time because tasks that used to take a long time can be done quickly and privately.

Second, our communication tools such as mistake-proof messages™ remove the emotional and inter-personal stressors in the workplace that often cause burnout.

Third, anyone in your company can easily conduct 360-degree evaluations. Outside consultants charge $500 or more per "evaluation" visit, often with less accurate results.
