what is 360notion™

Whether you are a startup or a big enterprise, you need to engage with your coworkers in order to get things done. After creating a 360notion™ network for your company, you can:


  • Share status updates
  • Communicate and share based on context
  • Privately chat with coworkers
  • Safely share and store files and intellectual property
  • Conduct 360-degree evaluations
  • Control your network with admin functions
  • and more value-added functions…
